Mushroom Bisque

Description

Mushroom Bisque is a velvety, indulgent soup that celebrates the earthy flavors of mushrooms. Sauteed with onions and garlic, the mushrooms create a rich and robust basem while a touch of thyme addes depth. Blended to a creamy texture and infused with either cream or a non-dairy alternative, this comforting bisque deights the palate with its smoothness. A drizzle of truffle oil or a sprinkle of chives elevates its presentation, making it a perfect choice for a luxurious appetizer or a comforting meal on its own.
